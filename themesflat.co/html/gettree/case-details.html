Transforming a Small Dairy Farm into a High-Yield Operation

Bodjames Agro-Allied Business Services was approached by a small dairy farm struggling with low milk production and poor animal health.
The farm, located in a rural area, had 50 dairy cows, but inconsistent feeding practices and inadequate housing were major issues.

Our Approach

We started with a comprehensive farm assessment, including soil testing, forage analysis

Results

Within six months, milk production increased by 40%, and the overall health of the herd improved

The company developed a customized feed plan for dairy cows, provided regular veterinary services, designed new housing facilities with proper ventilation and comfortable bedding, and installed modern milking equipment for efficient and hygienic milk collection, all aimed at improving the health and welfare of the animals.
A small dairy farm in a rural area approached Bodjames Agro-Allied Business Services with significant issues in milk production and animal health. The farm, with 50 dairy cows, was grappling with inconsistent feeding practices and inadequate housing, leading to low yields and poor cow health. This transformation not only boosted the farm's profitability but also improved the farmer's satisfaction with the operations.
